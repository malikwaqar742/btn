Back to Nature Yoga Chatbot

This demo page shows the friendly chatbot assistant designed for the Back to Nature website. It is responsive, animated, and built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

How to integrate

Include the stylesheet and script in your existing site pages, then the chatbot will automatically appear as a floating widget.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="css/chatbot.css">
<script src="js/chatbot.js" defer></script>
