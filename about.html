Our Story & Philosophy

About Back to Nature

Back to Nature was founded to help people rediscover their natural rhythm through yoga, gentle movement, and mindful connection with the outdoors.

Our Story

The journey began with a desire to blend yoga practices with the calm of nature. We created Back to Nature as a sanctuary where every student feels supported, refreshed, and inspired.

Mission

To promote healthy living and mindfulness through approachable yoga classes, compassionate guidance, and a community rooted in wellbeing.

Vision

Build a welcoming community focused on wellness, inner peace, and sustainable balance for every body and mind.

Nature-Inspired Wellness

We imagine yoga as a way to reconnect with simple natural rhythms, gentle movement, and the restorative power of breath.
